Welcome to India Untravelled. We’ll take you to offbeat and rural getaways in India that no one’s told you about. Our destinations offer unique cultural experiences, are socially responsible, and will make you fall in love with them.

There are lots of pre-conceived notions about village travel in India, but as fellow travellers, we can vouch for how enriching a rural experience can be. All you need to do is trust your gut, pack your bags, email us to book your accommodation, and set out, into an India Untravelled.

We work as a social enterprise that aims to set travellers off the beaten track in rural India, where life can be experienced through unique countryside escapes, organic farm stays, village stays, and traditional home stays.
