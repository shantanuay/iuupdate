2. How to Book
To reserve one of the partner destinations/itineraries/trips organised by India Untravelled, you should send an email to untravel@indiauntravelled.com or call on +91 9816 822 880.

3. Payment
The prices quoted are for payment by bank transfer, online transfer or cheque. The total price is payable to India Untravelled, 10 days prior to departure. Failure to pay by that date entitles India Untravelled to cancel your reservations without notice. Prices are quoted in Indian Rupees.

Incase of a conflict, the prices charged by the partner destinations and service providers involved will be final.

4. Cancellations
If it becomes necessary to cancel your booking, you must notify India Untravelled immediately in writing. Once we receive your notice, cancellation will take effect. Please note that the following charges will apply on cancellation:

  • if cancellation takes place more than 30 days prior to departure, your full deposit will be returned.
  • if cancellation takes place between 30 days & 15  days prior to departure, 50% of the booking price will be forfeited; and
  • if cancellation takes place less than 15 days prior to departure, 100% of the booking price will be forfeited.

If the tour/itinerary price is quoted as a package, no partial refunds or credit will be given for services not used. Please note that the above quoted amounts may change without notice. We also recommend that you obtain travel insurance upon booking.

*If there is a conflict between this cancellation policy & the service provider’s (homestay, farmstay, transport company etc) cancellation policy, the latter will prevail.
