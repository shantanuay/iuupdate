Away from the cities, lives an India where nature is still worshipped, food cooked on mud utensils, and visitors welcomed with open arms.

The pins on the map above point you to offbeat experiences in rural India. You can experience life in traditional village huts, on organic farm stays, and in heritage homestays, while rejuvenating yourself amid the bounty of nature. As you watch the spirit of rural India come to life in its folk music & dances, you’ll begin to form a connection with the people of the region, and crave for the simplicity of their lives.

To make the most of your adventures off the beaten path, lose your inhibitions about rural India, interact freely with your hosts, soak in the village culture, and do all the things you’ve always loved to do but never get the time to.
