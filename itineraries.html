Use our customizable itineraries to build your own travel trails.

Itineraries

  • Life on a Houseboat, Srinagar

    Spend a few days basking in the beauty that Kashmir is known for. Live off a houseboat on the scenic Dal Lake in Srinagar, feast on delicious Kashmiri cuisine and breathe in the heavenly sights while on Shikara rides across the lake

  • Goa Away From The Beaches

    Discover North Goa’s best-kept secrets in Aldona village - homestay with a Goan Portuguese family, go kayaking along Goa’s backwaters and experience the culture of Goa.

  • Darjeeling to Sikkim: Introduction to the Northeast Himalayas

    Deep in the interiors of the remote North-eastern Himalayas, India Untravelled takes you back in time in a village of small tea farmers near Darjeeling , and then transports you on foot through breath-taking landscapes, to the lost kingdom of Sikkim, the last state to be annexed by India in 1973.

  • Secrets of the Kumaon Himalayas

    Discover life in the Kumaon Himalayas of Uttarakhand, at charming heritage homestays and Himalayan villages near the Uttarakhand hill stations of Bhimtal, Almora, Mukteshwar & Binsar

    The Secrets of the Kumaon Himalayas trail starts at a 19th century heritage homestay in the tranquil lake district of Bhimtal. You continue into the heart of Kumaon, to stay in a charming mountain village near the Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary. And your trip ends at the colonial Dak Bungalow in the terraced valleys of Peora.

  • Best-Kept Secrets of Kerala

    Leave behind Kerala’s tourist circuit and discover the picturesque countryside in the north of the state. Pamper yourself in a Kerala of virgin backwaters, misty tropical forests, and sandy white beaches caressed by the waves of the Arabian Sea. Experience life in a postcard bamboo village, indulge your taste buds in the delicious local cuisine, wander along dense coffee, rubber and coconut plantations, and treat yourself to the warm hospitality of your host families. On this trail, you witness the many faces of Kerala, and immerse yourself in its culture, traditions and crafts.

    This itinerary takes you to small towns and villages in Wayanad, Kannur and Kasaragod, where local families host you.

  • Untravel Delhi

    India Untravelled invites you on a most exclusive discovery of India’s capital city, Delhi. Stay with a local family in a typical Delhi neighborhood. Hike amid the Aravalis to pristine lakes, delve deeper into the life of Tibetan people, and let the forgotten crafts of East Delhi amaze you. Take in a glimpse of life in village life in the city, and try your hand at pottery. You know that the city is home to 5,000 years of history and mouth-watering food, but give us a chance to show you the sights and sounds no guidebook will; untravel Delhi.

  • Magical Spiti

    Journey through the cold mountain desert of Spiti, nestled in the high Himalayas of India, in Himachal Pradesh. Experience life in the highest inhabited villages in the Himalayas, be awed by fascinating Himalayan landscapes, explore centuries old Buddhist trails in the region, spot endangered mountain flora & fauna, and in the process, help contribute to the responsible development & conservation of the valley. 

    This itinerary lets you stay in traditional Spitian homestays and in postcard Himalayan villages, where you can discover the magic of what we like to call “the Spiti experience”.
