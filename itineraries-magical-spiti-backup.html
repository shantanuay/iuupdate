Magical Spiti

Magical Spiti

Journey through the spectacular mountain desert of Spiti, experience life in the highest inhabited villages in the Himalayas, ride, hike & swim amid enchanting Himalayan landscapes, explore inspiring Buddhist trails in the region, spot endangered mountain flora & fauna, and help conserve the delicate ecology of the valley.

On this trip, you can expect:
  • Himalayan hospitality in traditional homestays.
  • Yak safaris in the high Himalayas.
  • Day hikes in Snow Leopard & Himalayan Wolf habitats.
  • An immersion into Spitian culture, music & dance.
  • Fascinating remnants of Himalayan history.
  • Mountain biking in some of the most stunning and rugged back country trails.
  • A glimpse into a well-preserved Buddhist heritage.
  • Local Spitian cuisine.
  • The chance to volunteer in on-going development & conservation projects.
  • The magic of what we like to call the Spiti experience.

Please note:

This is a carbon neutral trip in collaboration with an on-ground organization. It is our endeavour to generate minimum emissions on this journey, and whatever emissions we do generate are calculated and offset through investments in renewable energy projects within the region.

The revenues generated from your trip will contribute to the development and conservation of the region, as well as to further the cause of responsible travel in India. During your trip, you’ll be offered insights into the local development and conservation issues of the Trans-Himalayan region, and an overview of social initiatives undertaken to ensure sustainable development.

To plan your travel or book your stay, please email us at untravel@indiauntravelled.com
