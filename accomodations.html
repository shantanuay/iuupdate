Get Involved
     
Click on the map pins to read more about a destination

Away from the cities, lives an India where nature is still worshipped, food cooked on mud utensils, and visitors welcomed with open arms.

The pins on the map above point you to offbeat experiences in rural India. You can experience life in traditional village huts, on organic farm stays, and in heritage homestays, while rejuvenating yourself amid the bounty of nature. As you watch the spirit of rural India come to life in its folk music & dances, you’ll begin to form a connection with the people of the region, and crave for the simplicity of their lives.

To make the most of your adventures off the beaten path, lose your inhibitions about rural India, interact freely with your hosts, soak in the village culture, and do all the things you’ve always loved to do but never get the time to.

India Untravelled's current rural destinations include:

~ Prakriti Farm - "Traditional Punjabi Farm Stay, near Chandigarh"
~ Nirvana Organic Farm - "Rajasthani Village Farm Stay, near Jaipur"
~ Getaway Jungle Camp - "Eco Forest Camp in the Himalayas, Sattal"
~ Treasure Trove - "Coffee Plantation Homestay on Stilts, in Wayanad"
~ Oyster Opera - "Private Island Escape in the Backwaters of North Kerala"
~ Himalayan homestays in the mountain desert of Spiti
~ Bamboo Village, Wayanad - North Kerala
~ Dak Bungalow – Peora, Kumaon, Uttarakhand
~ Coorg - Discover life in Coorg with a local family, on an organic farm stay.
~ Bhimtal - Smetaceks' Colonial Homestay in Bhimtal, Uttarakhand.
~ Aldona, Goa - Amaral’s Goan-Portuguese homestay in Aldona, North Goa
~ Uttarakhand's Garhwal Himalayas
